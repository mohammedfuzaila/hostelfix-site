THE BACKSTORY

HostelFix was born out of frustration.

Imagine a hot summer night with a broken fan and a final exam tomorrow. The warden says wait 3 days. We said enough. HostelFix was created to bridge the gap between student problems and reliable local fixes.

From a single hostel room to 15+ universities, we've helped over 10,000 students live a more productive and stress-free life.

Mission

To empower every hostel student with instant, reliable, and affordable lifestyle services, making college life about the memories, not the chores.

Vision

To become the foundational infrastructure for student living globally, fostering a community where problems are solved in minutes.
